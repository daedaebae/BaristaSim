# Game Mechanics

## ‚òï Brewing
Mastering the art of brewing is the core of Chillista.

### 1. Pour Over / Regular Coffee
The classic brew.
1.  **Boil Water**: Click "Boil Water" and wait ~3s for the kettle to heat up.
2.  **Grind Beans**: Use standard or premium beans.
3.  **Add Water**: Pour the hot water over the grounds.
4.  **Stir**: Agitate the grounds for extraction.
5.  **Plunge**: Push through the filter.
6.  **Serve**: Hand it to the customer!
    > [!IMPORTANT]
    > **Water Temperature**: Always ensure water is **Hot** (Boiled) before adding! Serving coffee made with cold water results in a **Quality Penalty**.

### 2. Matcha Latte (Unlockable)
A calming tea ceremony. *Requires "Matcha Kit" upgrade.*
1.  **Sift Powder**: Prepare the fine matcha powder.
2.  **Add Water**: Add hot water (Don't forget to boil!).
3.  **Whisk**: Use the bamboo whisk to froth.
4.  **Serve**: A smooth green delight.

### 3. Espresso (Unlockable)
High pressure, high speed. *Requires "Espresso Machine" upgrade.*
1.  **Grind**: Fine grind for extraction.
2.  **Tamp**: Compress the puck.
3.  **Pull Shot**: Extract the espresso.
4.  **Steam Milk**: Froth the milk.
5.  **Pour Art**: Combine for a latte.

## üë• Customers
Customers arrive periodically based on your **Reputation** and the **Weather**.

### Patience
Each customer has a patience timer (Green -> Yellow -> Red).
-   **Cozy Mode**: Patience decays slowly.
-   **Extreme Mode**: Patience decays rapidly!
-   **Consequences**: If the timer hits zero, the customer leaves, and you lose Reputation (-5).

### Archetypes
-   **Regular**: Standard patience.
-   **Critic**: Low patience, but high reputation reward.
-   **Hipster**: Impatient, prefers Matcha/Pour Over.
-   **Student**: Moderate patience, tips well.
