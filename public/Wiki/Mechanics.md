## Basics
Serve customers the correct drinks before their patience runs out.

### Controls
- **Click** on ingredients to add them.
- **Click** "Brew" to start brewing.
- **Click** "Serve" when the order matches.

### Difficulty Levels
You change the difficulty in the **Settings** menu.
- **Cozy**: Time passes slower (0.5x). Relaxed customers.
- **Challenging**: Normal speed. Standard patience.
- **Extreme**: Fast time (1.5x). Impatient customers!

### Reputation
Serving customers correctly increases your reputation. Higher reputation unlocks new ingredients and locations!
